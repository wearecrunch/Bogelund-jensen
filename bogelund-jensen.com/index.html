About Bogelund-Jensen

All of the design ideas and draping techniques are developed in the atelier located at Strandvejen 59 in Copenhagen. Our Bøgelund-Jensen philosophy is that beauty comes from within – we are all individual in our expression, shape and temperament. Bøgelund-Jensen focuses on bringing personality out with our clothing. You are always welcome to come into the atelier for a talk about your needs, wants and personal adjustments.
